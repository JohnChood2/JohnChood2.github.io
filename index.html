Welcome to my personal professional webpage. Iam an observational and experimental astrophysicist with focuses in cosmology, millimeter transients (AGN) and instrumentation.

I am currently a NSF-OPP Fellow at the University of Chicago developing an observational pipeline that uses millimeter wavelength data from the South Pole Telescope and ACT to study the variability of Active Galactic Nuclei (AGN).

My research interests include:

  • Millimeter Transients (AGN)
  • MKID and TES detector development
  • Low loss microstrip materials for microwave applications
  • Astronomical Instrumentation
  • Experimental Cosmology
